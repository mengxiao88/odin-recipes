Sweet Sour Pork

Description

The recipe yields two large dinner-size bowls or four smaller ones.

Ingredients

  • Pork-the best cut (in order of preference): pork scotch (aka pork neck, pork collar), pork shoulder, leg, tenderloin then loin.
  • Baking soda (aka bi-carb) and cornflour/cornstarch> meat tenderising method widely used across Asia (notably in Chinese cooking) that is starting to be discovered by Western countries!
  • Garlic, ginger and onion>Grated to make them “juicy” so the flavour penetrates better
  • Soy sauce > - for seasoning and flavour

How to make Sweet and Sour Pork - OVERVIEW

  1. Marinate porkto infuse with flavour and tenderise the meat (we are using economical shoulder and scotch pork);
  2. Double dredgein cornflour/cornstarch, not flour, for extra crispy;
  3. Double fry>for extra crispy a trick Asians have been using for centuries;
  4. 10 second toss>to coat pork in Sweet and Sour Sauce!
