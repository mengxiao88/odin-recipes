Pho Beef Soup

Description

The recipe yields two large dinner-size bowls or four smaller ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yellow onion, quartered, divided
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can low-sodium beef broth (1¾ cups)
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can low-sodium chicken broth (1¾ cups)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 (1-inch) piece fresh ginger, thinly sliced (no need to peel)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 star anise pod
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 7 oz flat rice noodles
  • 8 oz very thinly sliced beef, such as flat iron or flank steak
  • Salt, to taste
  • ½ cup bean sprouts
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Hoisin sauce and sriracha, for serving

Assemble the pho

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions while the broth simmers.
  2. Once the broth is ready, briefly cook the noodles and beef in the broth and divide into bowls.
  3. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and beef, garnish with bean sprouts and fresh cilantro.
  4. Pass lime wedges, hoisin sauce, and sriracha sauce at the table.
